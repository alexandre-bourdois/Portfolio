About Me

Hey there! I'm Alexandre Bourdois, a 22-year-old enthusiast passionate about electronics, embedded systems, and web development. Alongside my formal education and work, I independently explore web development, showcasing my dedication to being a versatile professional.

I'm open to diverse opportunities and eager to contribute to stimulating projects. My adaptability and passion for learning make me enthusiastic about embracing new challenges in both electronics and web development.
